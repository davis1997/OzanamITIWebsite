About the Institute

Forty years of quiet, practical service in the Vincentian tradition.

Ozanam Private ITI is a charitable initiative of the Society of St. Vincent de Paul, Puthenpeedika Parish — the same Vincentian family that has served India since 1863. Since 1980, our small campus in Thrissur has trained generation after generation of Mechanic Motor Vehicle (MMV) trade students under the National Council for Vocational Training, Govt. of India.

NCVT Affiliated · Govt. of India 2-year MMV trade certificate, recognised nationally for jobs and apprenticeship.

Financial Support

No deserving student is turned away for money.

In the Vincentian tradition, charity is not paperwork. If a student wants to learn, we find a way — through merit grants, parish support, and community contributions.

Merit-Based Award

Students with strong SSLC results and consistent practical performance are eligible for fee concessions throughout the course.

Need-Based Support

Students from economically weaker families receive tuition support, books, and uniform assistance from the parish trust.

Community Contribution

SSVP conferences across Thrissur sponsor seats every year. If a student can't pay, the community quietly does — that's how Ozanam works.
